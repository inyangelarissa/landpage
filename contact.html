Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and services.

How can I apply for your programs?

You can apply for our programs through the Programs page on our website. Each program has specific application requirements and deadlines, which are detailed on the respective program pages.

Are your programs free for students?

Many of our core programs are free for eligible students. Some specialized programs may have nominal fees, but we offer scholarships and financial assistance to ensure that cost is not a barrier to participation.

How can I support your initiative as a business?

Businesses can support our initiative through corporate sponsorships, employee volunteer programs, in-kind donations, and more. Visit our Get Involved page or contact our partnership team for more information.

Do you offer accommodations for students with disabilities?

Yes, we are committed to inclusivity and provide various accommodations for students with disabilities. Please contact our support team to discuss specific needs and how we can best support you.

How can I become a mentor?

To become a mentor, visit our Get Involved page and complete the mentor application form. Our team will review your application and contact you to discuss the next steps in the process.
